About PKMC

Padmakanya Multiple Campus (PKMC) was established in 1951 (Aswin 2008 B.S.) as the first women’s campus of Nepal. Initially, its academic activities were brought into operation at the present location of Kanya School, Dillibazar. King Tribhuvan inaugurated the campus.

The campus has five buildings: two for classrooms and administration, one for computer lab and e-library, one dormitory for 200 students, and one old hostel planned for BHM program. Other spaces accommodate cafeteria and dance section with theater and class facilities.

The library requires expansion to meet academic needs with more books, journals, and e-library resources.

Courses

  • BIT – 8th Semester course in Information Technology
  • BCA – 6th Semester course in Computer Applications
  • M.A. English – Master's program in English Literature
  • Social Work – Undergraduate program in Social Work
